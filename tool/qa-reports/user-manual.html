Who does what — at a glance

M Member — the everyday club user

This is most people. You join events, complete tasks given to you, and take part in the club. You can't create events or change other members' accounts.

A Admin — the organizer (secretaries, treasurers, office bearers)

You do everything a Member does, PLUS you create events, assign tasks, approve work, mark attendance, and send announcements. You can add new members to the club.

P President — the top authority

You do everything an Admin does, PLUS you can remove members from the club and promote/demote the office bearers.

1. For everyone — First sign in

Before you do anything, you need an account. Your club admin creates it for you and shares your email and a first-time password.

1

Open the app.

You'll see the Agaram logo and "Tamil Ilakiya Mandram" — this is the splash screen. It takes 2 seconds.

2

Sign in with the email and password your admin gave you.

Type your email (e.g. you@college.edu) and the password. Tap Sign In.

3

(Optional) Change your password later.

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password? You'll get an email with a reset link.

Tip: The first time you sign in, Android may ask permission to send you notifications. Tap Allow so you get event and task alerts.
If it says "Email or password is incorrect" — check you typed the email exactly as the admin gave it. Passwords are case-sensitive.

2. If you are a Member Member

You see 5 tabs at the bottom of the screen:

IconTabWhat you do there
🏠HomeToday's Kural, this month's theme, upcoming events, your star count.
📅EventsAll club events — upcoming, ongoing, past.
✅TasksJobs assigned to you by admin. Upload proof when done.
📁WalletEvent receipts, bills, financial documents.
👤ProfileYour photo, name, phone. Sign out button.

A. View events and check in

1

Go to the Events tab.

Tap an event you're attending. You'll see its details, venue, time, and tabs for Attendance / Tasks / Gallery / Wallet.

2

On the day of the event, tap "Check in".

This opens the QR scanner. Point your phone's camera at the QR code the admin shows on their screen.

3

You're marked present. +2 stars.

You'll see a success message. Your name appears in the event's Attendance list.

Check-in window: You can scan the QR starting 3 hours before the event begins, until the event ends. Outside this time, it says "Check-in closed."

B. Complete a task

1

Go to the Tasks tab.

You see all jobs assigned to you — Pending (not done yet), Submitted (waiting for admin review), Approved (done, stars earned), Rejected (admin asks you to redo it).

2

Tap a pending task to open it.

Read the details. Do the work in real life (bring the banner, arrange chairs, etc.).

3

Tap "Upload proof" and choose a photo or PDF.

Take a picture of the completed work, or upload a document. Add a short note if you like.

4

Tap Submit. Wait for admin to approve.

When approved, you get +3 stars and a notification. If rejected, you'll see the admin's reason — fix it and submit again.

File limits: Proofs must be under 10 MB. If your photo is too big, the app will tell you before uploading.

C. Upload event photos (Gallery)

1

Open the event → Gallery tab.

Tap the camera icon in the corner.

2

Pick a photo from your phone.

Add a caption (optional). Tap Upload.

You can delete only photos you uploaded yourself. Admin can delete any photo.

D. Upload wallet documents (receipts, bills)

1

Open the event → Wallet tab.

Tap the upload icon. Choose PDF for bills/invoices, or image for photos of receipts.

2

Add a title (like "Tea stall bill") and upload.

E. See your ranking (Leaderboard)

Leaderboard shows the top 50 members by stars. Your row is highlighted. The top 3 are shown on podium with crown/silver/bronze.

F. Update your profile

1

Go to the Profile tab.

Tap your photo to change it. Edit your name or phone if needed.

What a Member CANNOT do
  • Create or edit events
  • Create or assign tasks to others
  • Approve or reject tasks (only admin can)
  • Mark someone else's attendance
  • Change your role, position, or star count yourself
  • Add or remove other members
  • Send club-wide notifications

3. If you are an Admin Admin

You have a different home screen — the Admin Dashboard — showing club stats, events, and pending task reviews. You also see every tab the Member does, plus extra buttons.

A. Create an event (or a meeting)

1

Go to Events tab → tap the + button.

2

Fill in: Title, Description, Venue, Date & time, Duration.

Choose Event or Meeting. Optionally upload a banner image (under 5 MB) and pick the monthly theme.

3

Tap Create.

Every member gets a push notification. The event appears in their Upcoming list. A local reminder is set 10 minutes before the event starts.

B. Start attendance — generate the QR code

1

Open the event on the day → Attendance tab.

2

Tap Start attendance (or Rotate QR if one is already running).

A fresh QR code appears. Put your phone on a table or project it. Members scan it to check in.

Rotate the QR if you suspect someone is sharing a screenshot. A fresh QR invalidates old ones instantly.

C. Mark attendance manually

If a member forgot to scan, open the event → Attendance tab → tap Mark manually → pick the member. They get +2 stars.

Deactivated members are blocked. The app will refuse to mark attendance for a deactivated account and tell you to reactivate them first.

D. Create and assign tasks

1

Open an event → Tasks tab → + Add task.

2

Fill in: Title, Description, Assignee (pick a member), Due date (optional).

3

Tap Create.

The assigned member gets a push notification. The task appears in their Tasks tab.

E. Review submitted tasks

1

Go to your Admin Dashboard → Review Queue.

You see all tasks with status "Submitted" across every event.

2

Tap a task to see the proof (photo/PDF) and the member's note.

3

Tap Approve or Reject.

If you reject, write a short reason — the member will see it and resubmit. If you approve, the member gets +3 stars and a "Task approved" notification.

F. Add a new member

1

Go to the Members area (from dashboard) → + Add member.

2

Fill in: Name, Email, Phone, Role (Member or Admin), Position.

3

Tap Suggest password or type one.

The app creates a unique, easy-to-read password. Copy it and share with the new member privately (WhatsApp, in person). They'll use it to sign in the first time.

Save the password when you see it. For security, the app won't show it again. If the member forgets, they can use "Forgot password?" to reset.

G. Deactivate or reactivate a member

A member who leaves the club, or who shouldn't sign in for a while, can be put on hold:

1

Find the member in the Members list → tap them.

2

Tap Deactivate.

They lose sign-in access immediately. Their existing data (tasks, attendance, stars) stays intact. To bring them back, tap Reactivate.

H. Change someone's role or position

From the member's profile → tap Change role or Change position. You can promote a member to Admin, or change someone's title (e.g. Secretary → Treasurer). Only the President can hand over the President badge.

I. Send an announcement to everyone

1

Go to Notifications → + Compose.

2

Pick the audience: All members, Admins only, or a single person.

3

Write title + body. Tap Send.

Everyone in the audience gets a push + a copy in their Notifications inbox.

What an Admin (non-President) CANNOT do
  • Promote anyone to President (or take President away)
  • Delete members permanently (only deactivate)

4. If you are the President President

You are an Admin with one extra badge: isPresident. In addition to everything an Admin does, you have two final authorities:

A. Remove a member from the club permanently

This is permanent. Only use it when a member has truly left the club. For short absences or punishment, use Deactivate instead — it's reversible. Deletion removes their Firestore profile; their sign-in is also disabled automatically so they can't come back without admin action.
1

Open the member's profile → tap Remove from club.

2

Confirm.

Their sign-in is disabled, their profile is deleted. Their past attendance, task approvals, etc. stay in the club's records (the events still show them as a past attendee).

B. Hand over the President role

When a new President is elected:

1

Open the NEW president's profile.

2

Change Position → President.

The app automatically sets their isPresident flag. Only the current President can do this.

3

Change your own Position to something else (e.g. Vice-President, Member).

This clears your President badge. From now on, the new President has the top authority.

Tip: You can only have one President at a time per club. Setting a new President's position to "President" automatically un-sets any previous one.

5. How to earn stars ⭐

Stars are how the club recognizes active members. The leaderboard ranks members by stars.

What you didStarsWho awards
Checked in to an event (QR or manual)+2Automatic when admin marks present
Task approved by admin+3Admin on approval
Task rejected0(resubmit to try again)
Your own stars can't be edited by you. The app protects the leaderboard — only admin-driven actions (attendance + task approvals) move your count.

6. Questions & problems

I forgot my password.

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password? Enter your email. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for a reset link.

I'm not getting notifications on my phone.

  1. Check Android Settings → Notifications → Agaram → make sure it's allowed.
  2. Sign out and sign back in. This re-subscribes you to all notification topics.
  3. If still nothing, tell your admin — they can re-send the event notification.

The QR scan says "invalid or expired".

The admin has probably rotated the QR. Ask them to show you the new one on their phone and try again.

The QR scan says "check-in opens 3 hours before".

You're early. Scan closer to the event start time. The window opens 3 hours before the scheduled start.

It says "account has been deactivated".

Your admin has put your account on hold. Ask them to reactivate it if this is a mistake.

A task I see in the list won't let me upload proof.

Check the status — if it's already Submitted or Approved, you can't change it. Only Pending and Rejected tasks accept new proof.

I uploaded a photo by mistake — can I delete it?

Yes, only if YOU uploaded it. Go to the Gallery tab, tap your photo, tap delete. Admins can delete anyone's photo.

The leaderboard says I have fewer stars than I expected.

Right now, attendance stars are credited when the admin marks attendance manually. If you only scanned the QR, the stars are recorded on the event but not yet added to your profile count until an admin syncs — this is by design for security. Ask your admin to double-check the attendance list.

I changed phones — what do I do?

Just install the app on the new phone and sign in with the same email and password. All your data (stars, tasks, history) comes with you — it's stored in the cloud, not on your phone.
